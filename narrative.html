Work History
Raphael Francisco Tobar Jr. was born on June 3, 1985. A digital artist native to Miami, Florida, he has made significant travelling and lived in other cities to educate himself on the history of art, but also the aesthetic spirit in contemporary society.

At a young age, Tobar became familiar with software used for creating digital art through his father's sign business. By the time he graduated from high school in 2005, he had an opportunity to live in Paris, France for seven months. While in Paris, he spent most of his time observing important cultural documents and artifacts in art and scientific history at the museums, while developing critical thinking skills by a reading of philosophical texts.

Returning to Miami the following year in May, he began to practice the animal figure with birds. By 2008, he eventually moved to Los Angeles to develop his artistic sensibilities, and for three years became acquainted with the newest digital techniques in the entertainment industry. He returned to Miami in 2011 applying a new artistic direction, and studied graphic design at Miami Int'l University of Art & Design.
